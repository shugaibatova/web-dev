Pasta

  • Time:40 min
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
egg 1
flour 150 g

Step 1: Mix egg and flour by hands.
Step 2: Leave the dough to rest for 30 min
Step 3: roll out the dough thinly and divide with a knife into pieces 11-12.
Step 4:let it boil for 5-6 minutes and add salt to the water.Done
