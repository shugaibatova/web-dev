Camping

Posted 2 years ago.

The heart of any camping trip is the campfire. Sitting in a circle, sharing stories, and laughing until your stomach hurts—this is where the magic happens. With classmates, it’s even more entertaining because someone always has a funny school memory to share.
A few highlights from our campfire time:
S’mores Contest: Who could create the most perfectly roasted marshmallow?
Storytime: The scary stories didn’t scare anyone, but the exaggerated delivery sure made us laugh.
Music: One classmate brought a guitar, and we had an impromptu sing-along under the stars.

Day at University

Posted 1 years ago.

The day began with a flurry of excitement. We all gathered early in the morning, backpacks packed with essentials—tents, food, and plenty of snacks. As we loaded everything into the cars, the air was buzzing with chatter and jokes. Everyone was in high spirits, excited about the day ahead. The journey to the campsite was just as lively. The cars were filled with loud music, laughter, and constant debates over the best campfire songs. When we finally arrived, the fresh air and scenic surroundings greeted us. The campsite was perfect—nestled near a small lake with towering trees offering shade. As we stepped out, we couldn’t help but feel a sense of freedom that only nature can provide.
